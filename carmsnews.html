About the publication

Carms News is the only fully bilingual newspaper in the county. It is published four times a year and is distributed free to 83,500 homes as well as customer service centres, libraries and leisure centres, GP surgeries, hospitals and local leisure attractions.

The paper is produced by members of the Carmarthenshire Local Service Board, which includes:

  • Carmarthenshire County Council
  • Coleg Sir Gâr
  • Hywel Dda Health Board
  • Mid and West Wales Fire and Rescue Service
  • The University of Wales Trinity Saint David
  • Carmarthenshire Association of Voluntary Services
  • Natural Resources Wales

Could your business benefit from reaching 80,000+ homes? Get in touch to find out about a range of advertising opportunities. Email carmsnews@carmarthenshire.gov.uk or call 01267 224654.

Carms News is produced at nil cost through sponsorship and advertising.

Each edition costs an average £17,136 to produce and distribute. The average costs are:

Print = £7,080

Distribution = £10,056
